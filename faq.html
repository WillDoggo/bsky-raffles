FAQ

What is this tool?

This tool is made for artists on BlueSky who run raffles for their followers. It facilitates the process of selecting a winner based on a variety of critera depending on the rules of the raffle.

How does it work?

When the artist draws a winner (or winners), this tool first fetches all of the replies, reposts, and likes on the raffle post. Afterwards, it filters everything down to a set of entrants who have satisfied all the requirements. Finally, it draws a random winner from that pool (or multiple winners, depending on the artist's preference).

What does the "Verified" thing mean?

Drawings can be made by anyone, even those who did not author the original raffle post. This is because this tool does not require authorizing to any accounts, by default.

The verification indicates that the author themselves did the drawing.
